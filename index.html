The Six Life Domains

Our platform is built around these six core domains that form the foundation of a good life plan.

Safe

Living in stable and secure environments where physical and emotional safety are prioritized.

Healthy

Maintaining physical and mental wellbeing through appropriate supports and personalized routines.

Engaged

Actively participating in meaningful activities that match interests and provide purpose.

Connected

Building and maintaining positive relationships with family, friends, and the community.

Independent

Developing skills to make informed choices and live with the right balance of autonomy and support.

Included & Heard

Being valued for unique contributions and having a voice in decisions that affect day-to-day life.
